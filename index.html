Short Biography

Haiqin Yang is a senior researcher at Ping An Life, where he establishes the Statistical Artificial Intelligence and Learning (SAIL) lab to provide natural language processing services. Meanwhile, he works as an adjunct associate professor in the Department of Decision Sciences and Managerial Economics, The Chinese University of Hong Kong.

His current research lies in developing machine learning technologies, specifically deep learning technologies for natural language processing. He received the 2018 Asia Pacific Neural Network Society (APNNS) Young Researcher Award and is recognized as the Most Influential Scholar Award Honorable Mention for outstanding and vibrant contributions to the field of AAAI/IJCAI between 2009 and 2019.

His publications are in Google Scholar or DBLP. If you would like to discuss any research problems, seek an intern or full-time researcher position, please send him an email.
